How to access Wilma

To access Wilma within the Terminal (via FTP), simply follow these steps:

  1. Open the Terminal.
  2. Enter this command with your own username: "ssh username@wilma.vub.ac.be".
  3. Enter your password.
  4. Enter this command: "su - se2_1314".
  5. Now you can navigate on the Wilma server.
